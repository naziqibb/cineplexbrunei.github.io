Introduction:
The Banana Leaf Catering Services was incorporated in the early year of 2015 with the sole purpose of providing good food to the general Bruneian with an affordable price. It manages to capture the Bruneian heart through its many delicious local delicacies namely its kuih variety, malay food, pineapple tarts, nasi lemak, rendang chicken, fried tom-yam meehoon and etc.
History:
The Banana Leaf Catering Services was founded by Hj Shahzeamy bin Hj Manan whom have dedicated so much of his time and energy into food industry. Business before The Banana Leaf Catering Services incorporation was then a home-made humble company with only a BND$200.00 start-up capital which produces kuih for wholesale to the morning market. Home-made and product originality was a strong word of mouth advertisement that contribute to its success.
Current:
Since then, business has improved by leaps and bounce. With support from its loyal customers and suppliers, it has extended its business from kuih wholesale to food catering business. Today, The Banana Leaf Catering Services is a company with multi-capability in producing all kinds of food for all kinds of events. Full moon package, birthday party, wedding, seminars, conference, house warming, opening ceremony..... name it all and The Banana Leaf Catering Services will be there to serve you with good food.
